About me

I am 5th year PhD student at Stevens Institute of Technology, advised by Eric Koskinen and Tegan Brennan. My work applies formal methods to reason about program correctness, with particular interest in programming languages, automated analysis and verification techniques, and concurrency.

Before beginning my PhD, I spent time as a consultant for the United Nations in Geneva, focusing on the design and development of distributed systems for e-government platforms.
